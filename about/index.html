About Me

Hey there!

Welcome, in case you don't know me already my name is Pedro G. Galaviz. I created this site in order to share some of my thoughts & interests with the world (more likely 2-3 people that randomly find it haha).

Some facts about me:

  • I was born and live in Guadalajara, Mexico.
  • I'm 31 years old.
  • I'm a musician, a guitarist to be more specific, used to play in a local band called Vida Boulevard, you can listen to our music on Spotify.
  • I LOVE Mexican food!
  • I've visited 16 countries counting Mexico (wish I have time to visit much more).
  • I run a small construction company and do freelance development for a living, I've always loved architecture, art and design in general. I proudly live in a house I designed and built.
  • Circumstances lead me to create a website about 7 years ago, since then I was hooked by Web Development & Programming in general.
  • I've created some web apps for small companies in Mexico & USA.
  • I'm an avid learner, I can't sleep without knowing I learned something new, for real!
  • I have worked with JavaScript, Ruby, Elixir/Erlang, and a little of Go.
  • My favorite author is the italian Umberto Eco.
  • I love herps (Reptiles & Amphibians), especially snakes, currently I have 14 and 2 Gila Monsters.
  • I'm looking forward to develop & launch some of my tech ideas in a future.
  • I'm an introvert (not to be confused with timid).
  • I can speak 3 languages. (sort of haha)

I'm always looking for a conversation, so if you have anything to tell me, suggest or ask you can always contact me on Twitter, Github, Instagram or send me an email to hello@pggalaviz.com.
